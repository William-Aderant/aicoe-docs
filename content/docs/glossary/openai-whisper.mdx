OpenAI Whisper is a general-purpose speech recognition model trained on a large dataset of diverse audio. It approaches human-level robustness and accuracy in English speech recognition and is capable of multilingual speech recognition, speech translation, and language identification.

## Key Features

- **Multilingual Support**: Recognizes and transcribes speech in multiple languages
- **Translation Capabilities**: Translates speech from various languages into English
- **Language Identification**: Automatically detects the language of spoken content
- **Robust Performance**: Handles accents, background noise, and technical language
- **Open Source**: Available under the MIT license for integration into applications

## Use Cases

- Audio and video transcription
- Multilingual speech recognition
- Real-time speech-to-text applications
- Podcast and meeting transcription
- Accessibility features for applications
